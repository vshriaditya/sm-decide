SAGEMAKER CUSTOMIZATION ADVISOR

Pick the right SageMaker customization technique before you train.

SageMaker offers four fine-tuning techniques and Nova Forge for deeper customization. Most engineers pick one without knowing what the others would have done. sm/decide walks you through the decision in under two minutes - showing its reasoning at every step.

What SageMaker doesn't tell you

No path guidance

SageMaker's Model Customization console assumes you've already decided between SFT, DPO, and RFT. There is no wizard, no recommendation, and no explanation of when each technique applies.

SFT won't teach new facts

A common mistake: using SFT to inject new domain knowledge into a model. AWS documentation explicitly states SFT changes behavior, not knowledge. For new facts, you need Nova Forge continued pre-training or RAG. sm/decide catches this before you waste a training run.

Nova Forge is deeper, not just different

Nova Forge isn't an alternative to fine-tuning - it's a deeper tier of it, starting from earlier model checkpoints. It requires HyperPod infrastructure and program approval. Most engineers don't know this distinction exists until they're already mid-project.
